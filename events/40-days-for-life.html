40 Days for Life (Mississauga)

Join a peaceful, prayerful vigil to end abortion—40 days of prayer & fasting, community outreach, and a loving presence outside abortion facilities. The next internationally coordinated campaign runs Wednesday, September 23 – Sunday, November 1, 2026.

Sign Up for Vigil Hours

Fall 2026 Campaign

Wednesday, September 23 – Sunday, November 1, 2026

40 Days for Life holds two coordinated campaigns each year: one during Lent (spring) and one beginning the fourth Wednesday of September (fall). The Spring 2026 campaign (February 18 – March 29) has concluded.

  • Daily vigil hours: 7:00 am – 7:00 pm
  • 40 consecutive days of prayer & fasting
  • Peaceful, lawful, public witness

Mississauga Vigil Location

In our community, the peaceful and prayerful vigil is held outside the Mississauga Women's Clinic at 101 Queensway Blvd, coordinated through local parishes including St. Martin of Tours.

Participants stand in silent prayer, offering help and hope to women facing difficult decisions. All vigils follow Ontario's safe-access zone laws—please follow campaign guidelines and coordinator instructions.

What it is

  • 40 days of prayer & fasting
  • Peaceful, lawful, public witness
  • Community outreach to offer help & hope

How to participate

Visit the official 40 Days for Life website to search for the Mississauga campaign, create or log in to your account, pick a vigil time, and follow campaign guidelines. You can also contact your local parish for volunteer sign-up sheets.
