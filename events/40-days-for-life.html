40 Days for Life (Mississauga)

Join a peaceful, prayerful vigil to end abortion—40 days of prayer & fasting, community outreach, and a loving presence outside abortion facilities. Official sign-ups and schedules are managed on the 40 Days for Life website.

Go to Mississauga Sign-Up

What it is

  • 40 days of prayer & fasting
  • Peaceful, lawful, public witness
  • Community outreach to offer help & hope

How to participate

Visit the official Mississauga page, create or log in to your 40 Days account, pick a vigil time, and follow campaign guidelines.

Visit Official Page

Local coordination

RTLMB supports promotion and volunteer mobilization; official scheduling is on 40DaysForLife.com.
