Life Chain (Canada)

An annual, peaceful, prayerful public witness for children killed by abortion—held in ~350 locations across Canada. Check the national page for the date, city listings, and local organizer details.

Find your Life Chain

What to expect (1 hour)

Stand in silent prayer with sign provided by local organizers; families welcome. This is a peaceful, prayerful witness that brings communities together to remember the children lost to abortion.

  • One-hour commitment
  • Signs provided by organizers
  • Family-friendly event
  • Silent prayer and reflection

When is the next Life Chain?

Sunday, October 5, 2025 (2–3 pm in most locations). Always verify your city's listing on the official Campaign Life Coalition website.

How to participate

Use the CLC page to find your location/time and organizer details; bring water/weather-appropriate clothing; arrive 10 minutes early.

  • Find your local organizer
  • Dress for the weather
  • Bring water and arrive early
  • Follow organizer instructions
